### 2. Engagements (Ambition) `/engagements/`

**Layout Structure:**
1. Manifesto headline section
2. Five belief statements (scrolling text blocks)
3. Three core ambition cards with icons

**Manifesto Headline:**
> "CHEZ ACTUAL, NOUS CROYONS À LA FORCE DU COLLECTIF"

**Five Belief Statements:**
1. Trust in current champions and emerging talents
2. Support for coaches, trainers, and athlete development staff
3. Commitment to healthcare, nutrition, and facility management professionals
4. Belief in supporters who encourage athletes
5. Recognition that all sports performance requires collective teamwork

**Three Core Ambitions (displayed as icon cards):**

| Ambition | Icon Label | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Collective Performance | AMBITION-COLLECTIF | Sports performance requires broad organizational support beyond visible athletes. Actual wants to be a meaningful "jersey," not just a corporate logo. |
| Values Over Visibility | AMBITION-VALEURS | Focus on sports values (effort, perseverance, respect, responsibility, courage, fraternity) rather than audience ratings. |
| Local Anchoring | AMBITION-LOCAL | High-level sports require strong local foundations. Actual's regional agencies embody localized, human-centered operations. |

## Content Strategy

### Themes
- **Collective strength** — recurring message across all pages
- **Values alignment** — sport and employment share the same human values
- **Local anchoring** — territorial presence and community identity
- **Inclusion** — para-athletes, diversity, youth development

### Content Types
- Long-form partnership stories
- Video series (Heritage Rugby)
- Athlete profiles with professional photography
- News articles (primarily sailing-focused: 24 of ~34 articles)
- Technical specs (boat data)
- Team rosters

### Tone
- Inspirational, corporate, values-driven
- French language only (no English version)
- Quote-heavy — athlete and executive testimonials throughout
